ZhongKe Fastener Co., Ltd. is a specialized manufacturer of industrial fasteners based in Dongtai, Jiangsu — one of China's most concentrated manufacturing corridors. Since 2005, we have grown from a single-line workshop into a fully integrated operation that handles everything in-house: cold heading, CNC turning, thread rolling, heat treatment, surface finishing, and automated optical inspection.

What We Make

  • Hex bolts, flange bolts, carriage bolts, U-bolts
  • Hex nuts, lock nuts, flange nuts, cap nuts, weld nuts
  • Self-tapping screws, machine screws, wood screws
  • Flat washers, spring washers, lock washers, sealing
  • Blind rivets, solid rivets, drive rivets, lockbolts
  • Dowel pins, taper pins, cotter pins, spring pins
  • Custom fasteners — any drawing, any material

Standards & Certifications

  • ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management
  • DIN / ISO / GB / ANSI / BS / JIS compliance
  • CE Certified for EU market entry
  • RoHS & REACH compliant materials available
  • Full Material Test Certificates (MTC) with every order
  • PPAP Level 1-3 available on request
  • Third-party inspection: SGS, TUV, Bureau Veritas

14   Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What is your minimum order quantity?
Standard parts start at 500–1,000 pcs per size. Custom parts typically 200–500 pcs. We're flexible on trial orders for new customers — contact us with your requirements.
Q: Do you provide free samples?
Yes — free samples of standard products (you cover shipping). For custom parts, a small tooling fee applies, refundable on your first production order.
Q: How long does shipping take?
Standard parts ship in 5–7 working days; custom parts in 15–25 days. Sea freight adds 25–40 days depending on destination. Air freight is 5–8 days. Express (DHL/UPS/FedEx) is 3–5 days.
Q: Can you match my existing supplier's price?
We're competitive on quality-for-price. Send us your current spec and target price — we'll tell you honestly what we can do. Often we can match or beat pricing while providing better documentation and service.
Q: What if parts arrive defective?
We ship with full inspection reports and MTCs. In the rare case of a quality issue, we replace or refund — no questions asked. Our OTIF (on-time, in-full) rate is 98.5%.
Q: Do you offer OEM / private labeling?
Yes — custom packaging, branded labels, laser marking on fastener heads, and custom carton printing are all available.
Q: What surface treatments are available?
Zinc (clear/blue/yellow/black), Hot-dip galvanized, Dacromet/Geomet, Black oxide, Phosphate, Nickel, Chrome, Passivation (stainless), and more. Tell us your operating environment and we'll recommend the right finish.
Q: Are you RoHS / REACH compliant?
Yes — all products can be supplied RoHS (2011/65/EU) and REACH compliant upon request. We maintain a database of compliant materials and can provide compliance statements with your order.
Q: How do I get a quote?
Email sales@zhongke-fastener.com with: (1) product name, (2) size/spec, (3) material & grade, (4) surface finish, (5) quantity. We'll reply with pricing, lead time, and shipping options within 24 hours.
Q: Can I visit your factory?
Absolutely. We're located in Dongtai, Jiangsu — about 3 hours from Shanghai. We welcome factory visits and audits. Please schedule in advance so we can arrange transportation and prepare for your visit.
